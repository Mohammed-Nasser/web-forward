Name: mist.io
----
Info: mist.io helps you manage and monitor your virtual machines on multiple public and private clouds using your mobile phone, tablet or laptop. Create, destroy, reboot and tag virtual machines and - more importantly - execute shell commands through the web, using an interface optimized for touchscreens.
